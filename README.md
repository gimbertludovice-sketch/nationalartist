# Federico Aguilar Alcuaz — National Artist Dashboard

An interactive one-page website about **Federico Aguilar Alcuaz**, National
Artist of the Philippines for Visual Arts (Painting, Sculpture, and Mixed
Media).

## What's inside

- **Home** – introduction and National Artist badge
- **Biography** – birth, family, education, and career timeline
- **Achievements** – full name, National Artist title, languages, and specialties
- **Works** – sample artworks and a gallery of other notable pieces
- **Contributions** – his contributions to Philippine art and list of awards
- **Legacy** – his lasting impact on Philippine and international art

## How to open

Just double-click the `.html` file and it will open in your browser — no
internet or installation needed.
